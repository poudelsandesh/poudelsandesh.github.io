About Me

Sandesh Poudel is a Ph.D. student and Graduate Research Assistant in the Department of Agricultural and Biological Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). His research focuses on the intersection of robotics, artificial intelligence, remote sensing, and precision agriculture and aims to develop innovative solutions for sustainable agricultural systems...
